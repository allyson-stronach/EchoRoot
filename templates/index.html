Can machine learning techniques uncover signs of sex trafficking in online sex ads?

-BLAH-

15,000-75,000 men, women, and children are trafficked into the U.S. each year.

Of suspected human trafficking in the U.S., 82% is for sex.

-BLAH-

According to the Thorn Survivor Survey, 3 out of 4 underage sex trafficking victims are advertised online.

The internet has simplified illegal transactions for johns, and continues to create new opportunities for pimps.

Backpage.com is one of the most ubiquitous sex ad websites in the world.

-BLAH-

EchoRoot applies a foundational machine learning concept, the Bayesian classifier, to the text of backpage.com ads in search for salient features that indicate human trafficking.
